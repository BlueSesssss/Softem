Your Soft Spot for Effective Study Time Management

Welcome to SoFTeM

SoFTeM (School/Student Friendly Time Management), or the "soft spot" where you can study comfortably, is designed to help students manage their time effectively and study more efficiently. Whether you're balancing homework, study sessions, or part-time jobs, SoFTeM helps you organize your time to maximize your study efforts.

This software provides intuitive tools to track time, set study goals, and optimize your daily schedule, ensuring you stay on top of your academic responsibilities without feeling overwhelmed.

Start Using SoFTeM

Track Time

The software helps students monitor how much time they spend on each study session, allowing them to track their productivity and identify areas for improvement.

Study Efficiently

Offers tools to optimize study habits, like setting timers for focused study intervals, minimizing distractions, and breaking tasks into manageable chunks.

Task Prioritization

Helps students prioritize their tasks based on deadlines, importance, or personal study goals, ensuring they stay focused on what's most urgent or valuable.

Goal Setting

Allows students to set academic goals (short-term or long-term), such as completing assignments or preparing for exams, and track progress over time.

Customizable Study Plans

Enables users to create personalized study schedules based on their preferred study times, subjects, and goals.

Study Break Reminders

Built-in reminders to take regular breaks, helping students maintain focus and avoid burnout during long study sessions.

Beneficial to All Ages

SoFTeM can help with academic or job problems and help you control your time in any workspace.
